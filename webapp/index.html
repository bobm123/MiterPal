In the workshop Pre-cut checklist
Mark the "outside face" on every blank before cutting.
Cut a test piece on scrap first — catch sign / edge-up mistakes.
Both edges get the same bevel and miter; flip the board end-for-end between cuts.
If your saw measures miter from the blade, use the complement (M′).
In the workshop Cut & fold checklist
Used units other than millimetres? Rescale the SVG in your cutter software before cutting (it's exported as 1 unit = 1 mm).
Score the dashed lines first, then cut the outline — the sheet stays rigid longer.
Every fold bends the same way; fold with the printed lines inside so they're hidden on the finished model.
Crease all folds sharply before any gluing.
Glue tabs under the mating panel, one joint at a time — the slits close up as the sphere rounds out. Joints without a tab glue edge-to-edge.
Building from modules: where two copies meet, each brings a tab — trim one of them.
